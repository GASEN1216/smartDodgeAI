[h1]智能躲避 AI (Smart Dodge AI)[/h1]

[h2]中文 (Chinese)[/h2]
还在为你的弹幕总是轻易命中敌人而感到无趣吗？想让战斗更具挑战性吗？

这个 Mod 赋予了泰拉瑞亚世界中的敌怪和 Boss 智能躲避玩家弹幕的能力！它们会尝试闪避你的攻击，同时保留原有的攻击和移动模式，让战斗更加动态和刺激。

[b]主要功能:[/b]
*   [b]智能闪避:[/b] 大部分敌怪和 Boss (除了城镇 NPC) 会尝试闪避玩家的弹幕和非纯近战武器。
*   [b]战术性瞬移:[/b] 当敌怪在闪避后，如果它正在冲向你，它会智能地瞬移到你身后继续追击！(此功能可配置)
*   [b]视觉预警:[/b] 敌怪瞬移前，目标位置会提前0.5秒出现残影，提供躲避时机。
*   [b]保留原版 AI:[/b] 闪避逻辑附加在原版 AI 之上，敌人仍然会正常攻击和移动。
*   [b]高度可配置:[/b] 通过游戏内的 Mod 配置菜单，你可以自由调整：
    *   全局闪避几率。
    *   按伤害类型（远程、魔法、召唤、近战）设置独立的闪避率。
    *   按特定弹幕类型设置更高优先级的闪避率。
    *   是否显示"MISS"文本、播放音效、显示粒子效果。
    *   是否启用 Boss 和普通敌怪的闪避功能。

[b]兼容性:[/b]
*   设计上兼容原版和其他大部分 Mod。
*   不直接修改原版 AI，减少冲突可能性。

[b]反馈与建议:[/b]
欢迎在 Steam Workshop 评论区或 GitHub 仓库提出任何问题或建议！

[h2]English[/h2]
Tired of your projectiles always hitting enemies too easily? Want more challenging battles?

This Mod gives enemies and bosses in the Terraria world the ability to intelligently dodge player projectiles! They will try to evade your attacks while maintaining their original attack and movement patterns, making combat more dynamic and exciting.

[b]Main Features:[/b]
*   [b]Smart Dodging:[/b] Most enemies and bosses (except town NPCs) will attempt to dodge player projectiles and non-pure melee weapons.
*   [b]Tactical Teleport:[/b] After dodging, if an enemy is charging at you, it will intelligently teleport behind you to continue the chase! (This feature is configurable)
*   [b]Visual Cue:[/b] Before an enemy teleports, an afterimage appears at the target location 0.5 seconds in advance, providing a window to dodge.
*   [b]Preserved Vanilla AI:[/b] The dodging logic is added on top of the vanilla AI, enemies will still attack and move normally.
*   [b]Highly Configurable:[/b] Through the in-game Mod config menu, you can freely adjust:
    *   Global dodge chance.
    *   Individual dodge rates for specific damage types (Ranged, Magic, Summon, Melee).
    *   Higher-priority dodge rates for specific projectile types.
    *   Whether to show "MISS" text, play sound effects, or show particle effects.
    *   Whether to enable dodging for bosses and normal enemies.

[b]Compatibility:[/b]
*   Designed to be compatible with vanilla and most other Mods.
*   Does not directly modify vanilla AI, reducing the possibility of conflicts.
